About Us

Premium jetski rides on Kenya's South Coast.

Welcome to Aqua Jetski Diani, a premium water sports experience at Golden Sun Resort. We offer exciting jetski rides for all levels, from quick 15-minute speed sessions to full dolphin tours and Chale Island adventures.

Whether you are visiting the Kenyan coast for the first time or looking for one of the best things to do in Diani, our team keeps every ride smooth, safe, and unforgettable.

All rides include a safety briefing, life jackets, and professional guides so you can focus on the ocean experience.

Questions

Everything you need to know before you ride.

Where are you located?

Aqua Jetski Diani is based at Golden Sun Resort, Diani Beach, Kwale County, Kenya.

How much do your jetski rides cost?

Our rides start from KES 4,000 for 15 minutes and go up to KES 21,000 for the Chale Island tour.

Do I need experience to ride?

No. Our rides are beginner friendly, and we provide a short briefing and simple instructions before you start.

Can two people ride on one jetski?

Yes. Our jetskis are built for two, so you can enjoy the ride with a partner, friend, or family member.

Do you offer dolphin tours?

Yes. Our 1 Hour Dolphin Tour is one of our most popular experiences for guests who want a scenic ride and a chance to spot dolphins.

How do I book a ride?

You can book through the form on this page or contact us directly on WhatsApp for quick assistance.

What should I bring?

Bring your towel, sunscreen, swimwear, and good energy. We provide life jackets and guidance.

Is it safe for children?

Child safety depends on age, confidence in the water, and guide advice on the day. Please contact us before booking so we can recommend the safest option.
