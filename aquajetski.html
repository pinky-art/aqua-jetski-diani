Questions

Everything you need to know before you ride.

What do I need to come with?

Just your towel, sunscreen, and vibes.

Do we have guides?

Yes, our professional and experienced guides are always on-site to help ensure your safety while you enjoy an exciting jetski ride.

Do you need experience while riding?

No experience needed. Our team will guide you through a short safety briefing and provide simple instructions before your ride. It is beginner-friendly and safe for everyone.

Do we provide life jackets?

Yes, we provide life jackets for all riders to ensure maximum safety while you enjoy your time on the water.

Can they ride with someone else?

Yes, our jetskis are built for two, so you can share the experience with a friend or family member.

Are your jetskis safe and well maintained?

Our jetskis are well-maintained, regularly serviced, and built to be safe and reliable, giving you a smooth and enjoyable experience on the water.

How do I pay for my booking?

Payments are made via M-Pesa, card payment, or cash.
